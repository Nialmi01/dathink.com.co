## 🎯 Objective
Analyze and improve the website https://dathink.com.co to:
- Rank higher on Google (SEO)
- Increase organic traffic
- Improve conversion rate (turn visitors into leads)
- Position the brand as an authority in automation and AI solutions for businesses

---

## 🧠 Context
Dathink is a company focused on:
- Process automation
- AI solutions (chatbots, WhatsApp automation, etc.)
- Custom software development
- Digital transformation for businesses

Target audience:
- Small and medium businesses (SMBs)
- Companies looking to reduce costs and automate processes

---

## 🔍 Tasks

### 1. SEO Audit (Technical + On-Page)
Analyze and fix:

- Missing or weak meta titles and descriptions
- H1, H2, H3 structure issues
- Lack of keyword optimization
- URL structure improvements
- Internal linking opportunities
- Image alt attributes
- Sitemap.xml and robots.txt
- Page speed improvements (Core Web Vitals)

---

### 2. Keyword Strategy
Define and map keywords for each page.

Focus on high-intent keywords such as:

- "automatización de procesos Colombia"
- "automatización empresarial Medellín"
- "bots de WhatsApp para empresas"
- "software a medida Colombia"
- "inteligencia artificial para empresas"

Assign:
- 1 primary keyword per page
- 3–5 secondary keywords

---

### 3. Content Expansion
For each main service page:

- Expand content to 1000–1500 words
- Include:
  - Problem → Solution → Benefits
  - Real use cases
  - Clear value proposition (ROI, cost reduction)
  - FAQs (important for SEO)

Each article must include:
- SEO title
